about us

MISSION

The South Bacolod General Hospital will provide a high standard and quality health and medical care to all patients in Bacolod City and the entire province of Negros Occidental by competent and capable as well as compassionate medical specialists and professionals.

VISSION

The South Bacolod General Hospital & Medical Center in the Island of Negros that will provide affordable and quality specialized hospital services and care to the people of Negros and to create avenues for training. These specialized medical services will be made readily available for all so that the people of Negros need not go out for specialized attention.
